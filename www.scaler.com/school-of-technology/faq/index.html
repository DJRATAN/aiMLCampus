The scholarship application process consists of the following steps:

1. Screening: Applicants are evaluated based on their academic performance, NSET scores, and a recorded personal interview.
2. Document Submission: Candidates need to submit the required supporting documents based on the specific category of scholarship they are applying for.
3. Sponsor's Assessment: Once the applicant's profile, including the recorded personal interview, statement of purpose (SOP), and supporting documents, is complete, it is shared with potential sponsors. NSET

The National Scaler Scholarship & Entrance Test is designed to evaluate candidates in three distinct sections, each with its own cutoff score. The test duration is 2 hours, and the sections included are:

1. Mathematics: The mathematics section evaluates the candidate's knowledge and problem-solving skills. It gauges their quantitative aptitude and ability to apply mathematical concepts to solve problems.
2. Aptitude and Logical Reasoning: This section measures the candidate's logical reasoning skills, analytical thinking, and problem-solving abilities.
3. Learnability Section: This unique section focuses on assessing the candidate's innate ability to learn new topics and quickly implement them. It aims to gauge their adaptability, grasping power, and readiness to learn and apply new conceptsan essential quality for success in the computer science field.

The entrance test evaluates the candidates' understanding of these subjects and their potential to excel in the field of computer science.
The NSET test prep kit will be made available on our website soon. Once you have completed your application and selected an NSET slot, you will receive the prep kit.

4. Different Approaches to Solving Each Section: Explore different approaches to tackle each section of the NSET. Techniques like skimming through the paper and planning, tackling easier questions first, or focusing on4 more challenging ones can be employed based on your strengths and preferences. Find the approach that works best for you.
5.Additional Study Resources: Supplement your preparation by referring to other relevant study materials. These can offer a more comprehensive understanding of the topics and help you identify areas where you need to focus more.

Remember to allocate sufficient time for practice and revision. Consistent effort and a systematic study plan will contribute to your success in the Scaler NSET. Good luck with your preparation!

Program

Our curriculum at Scaler School of Technology is designed to provide learners with a comprehensive skill set equivalent to that of a Senior Engineer/SDE-II, along with over a year of industry experience. The curriculum is divided into three phases, each focusing on different aspects of computer science and software engineering.

Initial 2 Years: During this phase, learners dive into and master the fundamentals of Data Structures and Algorithms (DSA), Fullstack development, Shell scripting, Frontend technologies, and essential soft skills. This foundation equips learners with a solid understanding of core concepts and practical skills needed for a successful career in the field.
Next 1 Year: In the second phase, learners have the opportunity to engage in a 1-Year Industry Internship at a Top Tech Company. This invaluable experience allows learners to apply their knowledge in a real-world professional setting, gain hands-on experience, and develop industry-specific skills. At the end of the internship, learners can also receive a Pre-Placement Offer (PPO), acknowledging their performance and potential for a full-time position.
Final 1 Year: The third phase focuses on specialization in high-demand fields such as Artificial Intelligence (AI), Machine Learning (ML), Algorithmic Trading, and other emerging technologies. Learners delve deeper into advanced concepts, tools, and techniques to become experts in these specialized domains. By the end of this phase, learners are prepared to secure placement offers from prominent Tech Giants, further accelerating their career growth.

Our curriculum is carefully crafted and regularly updated to align with industry trends and demands. It ensures that students receive a well-rounded education, combining theoretical knowledge, practical skills, and real-world experience, setting them up for success in the competitive tech industry.
