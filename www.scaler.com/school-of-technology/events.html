AI Engineering Specialization Programme
Turn Your Computer Science Dreams into Reality 

About this Programme:

The realm of artificial intelligence (AI) is reshaping entire industries, revolutionizing the way businesses leverage data to drive decision-making processes. In this fast-evolving landscape, organizations strive to stay competitive by harnessing the expertise of qualified AI engineers proficient in cutting-edge methodologies such as machine learning algorithms and deep learning neural networks.

Throughout the program, you'll have the opportunity to apply your knowledge by tackling industry-specific challenges using popular machine learning and deep learning libraries, including SciPy, ScikitLearn, Keras, PyTorch, and TensorFlow. From object recognition and computer vision to text analytics and natural language processing (NLP), you'll gain hands-on experience in solving real-world problems across diverse domains.

Moreover, you'll develop essential data science skills by scaling machine learning algorithms on big data platforms such as Apache Spark. Additionally, you'll explore various deep architectures, including convolutional neural networks, recurrent networks, and autoencoders, building, training, and deploying them to address complex challenges.

Throughout the program, you'll compile a portfolio of projects showcasing your mastery of course topics. These hands-on projects will provide you with practical experience working with machine learning libraries and deep learning frameworks, reinforcing your understanding of concepts and methodologies. 

Programme Syllabus
  • Introduction to Machine Learning
    • Learn the fundamentals of machine learning, including supervised and unsupervised learning.
    • Gain proficiency in Python programming for machine learning tasks.
  • Regression
    • Understand regression models and their applications.
    • Learn simple linear regression, multiple linear regression, and model evaluation techniques.
  • Classification
    • Explore classification algorithms such as K-Nearest Neighbours and decision trees.
    • Understand how to build and evaluate classification models.
  • Linear Classification
    • Dive deeper into logistic regression and support vector machines for classification tasks.
  • Clustering
    • Learn about clustering algorithms and their applications.
    • Master the k-Means clustering technique.
  • Deep Learning & Neural Networks with Keras
    • Gain an understanding of deep learning concepts and neural networks.
    • Learn about gradient descent, backpropagation, and activation functions.
    • Build and train deep learning models using Keras.
  • Computer Vision and Image Processing
    • Explore computer vision fundamentals and image processing techniques.
    • Learn about image classification, object detection, and neural networks for image tasks.
  • Deep Neural Networks with PyTorch
    • Understand the basics of PyTorch and tensor operations.
    • Learn about building and training deep neural networks using PyTorch.
  • Building Deep Learning Models with TensorFlow
    • Gain proficiency in TensorFlow and eager execution.
    • Learn about convolutional neural networks (CNNs), recurrent neural networks (RNNs), and other advanced deep learning concepts.
  • Learning Outcomes
    • Develop a strong foundation in machine learning and deep learning techniques.
    • Gain practical experience in building and training various machine learning models.
    • Acquire skills in using popular deep learning libraries such as Keras, PyTorch, and TensorFlow.
    • Learn how to apply machine learning and deep learning algorithms to real-world problems.
Learning Outcomes

  • Researchers and academics interested in advancing their knowledge and expertise in the field.
  • Entrepreneurs and business owners aiming to integrate AI and machine learning into their products and services.

Eligibility and Prerequisites

Eligibility

  • Educational Background:
    • A bachelor's degree in a relevant field such as Computer Science, Information Technology, Engineering, Mathematics, or Physics is typically required.
    • Candidates with equivalent practical experience in programming, data analysis, or software development may also be considered.
  • Professional Experience:
    • Prior work experience in related fields can be beneficial but is not always mandatory.
    • Experience in programming, data analysis, software development, or related areas is advantageous.

Prerequisites

  • Mathematics:
    • A solid understanding of linear algebra, calculus, probability, and statistics.
    • Knowledge of mathematical concepts such as matrices, vectors, differentiation, and integration is crucial for understanding machine learning algorithms.
  • Programming Skills:
    • Proficiency in Python programming is essential, as it is the primary language used in this specialization.
    • Familiarity with libraries and frameworks such as NumPy, pandas, scikit-learn, and Matplotlib.
  • Fundamentals of Machine Learning:
    • Basic knowledge of machine learning concepts, including supervised and unsupervised learning, model training, and evaluation.
    • Understanding of core algorithms and techniques such as linear regression, classification, and clustering.
  • Data Analysis:
    • Ability to preprocess and analyze data, including cleaning, transforming, and visualizing datasets.
    • Experience with data manipulation tools and techniques.
  • Tools and Technologies:
    • Familiarity with popular machine learning and deep learning libraries such as TensorFlow, Keras, and PyTorch.
    • Understanding of version control systems like Git.

Recommended Background

  • Software Development:
    • Experience in software development and software engineering principles.
    • Knowledge of software development life cycle, version control, and collaborative coding practices.
  • Database Management:
    • Understanding of database systems, SQL, and data retrieval techniques.
  • Domain Knowledge:
    • Domain-specific knowledge can be beneficial, depending on the area of application (e.g., healthcare, finance, e-commerce).

By meeting these eligibility criteria and prerequisites, candidates will be well-prepared to succeed in the AI Engineering Specialization program and gain the skills necessary to pursue opportunities in the field of artificial intelligence and machine learning.
