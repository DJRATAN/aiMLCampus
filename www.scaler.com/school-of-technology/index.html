After completing their specialization, students undergo a 6-month specialization and a 6-month internship in their chosen field to apply their skills practically in a company.
The number of students per batch in each respective Specialization program is 45.
To apply for our Specialization Programmes at AiML Campus, please follow these steps:
  • Visit our official website and navigate to the "Admissions" section.
  • Select the Specialization Programme you are interested in.
  • Review the admission requirements and ensure you are in the form.
  • Complete the online application form with accurate information..
  • Submit And Pay the application fee
  • Await notification regarding the status of your application.
  • If accepted, follow the instructions provided for enrollment and further steps.
For any additional queries or assistance with the application process, feel free to contact our admissions office. here.

  • Certification accredited by MACUL and recognized by AICTSD.

  • Endorsement certificates from respective companies.
  • Guidance from experienced faculty and industry experts.
  • Enhanced career prospects and networking opportunities.
