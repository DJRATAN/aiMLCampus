Step 1
Complete the Application Online
Submit your detailed profile that covers your personal details, academic performance & eligibility for scholarships.
Step 2
Block A Slot For The Scaler National Scholarship & Entrance Test (Scaler NSET)
Select one of the available slots. Check the Important Dates section for application fees & other information about the intake rounds.
Step 3
Appear For ScalerNSET
The 120-minute online test assesses the aptitude and growth mindset of the candidates. The test is strictly proctored using AI and has 4 sections to evaluate skills required for computer science. Details about the test are mentioned in the exam details section.
Step 4
Personal Interview Round
Once you qualify based on the Scaler NSET score, a 1:1 interview will be scheduled based on the available slot. Candidates are expected to review their application well.

The round will help us evaluate candidateâ€™s motivation, future aspirations and how they plan to make the most of this opportunity. This helps us consider the fitment of the candidate for this programme.
Step 5
Admission & Scholarship Application Results
Once the interview process is complete for shortlisted candidates, the admissions team will provide a final decision within 30 days.
Candidates will be placed into three categories: accepted, rejected, or waitlisted.
Accepted
For those who are accepted
Based on the ScalerNSET score and personal interview, admitted students will be given an admission offer. Scholarship decisions will also be communicated along with the admission results.
Waitlisted
For those who are waitlisted
Based on our internal ranking system, candidates could be waitlisted. Admission depends on the number of seats available for the cohort, and the admission committee will regularly reconvene to review the waitlisted candidates and roll out admission offers.
Rejected
For those who are rejected
Rejected candidates are encouraged to reapply in the following year. However, please note that application in the future rounds for the same academic year is not allowed.
Scaler will assist students to apply for the BSc in Computer Science programme offered by BITS Pilani once their admission is confirmed.

Unlocking Diverse Opportunities
Along with Scaler School of Technology's UG program, valued by top tech companies, our students would be advised to pursue a Bachelorâ€™s and Masterâ€™s degree in parallel to ensure they have more flexibility with their career outcomes.

By doing this, we want to bring the best of both worlds together. On the one hand, our learners will have access to the latest curriculum, be taught by teachers with practical experience, and pursue a 1-year tech internship that ensures they are set up for success in Computer Science in India & abroad.

On the other hand, formal degrees from a reputed university enable them to be eligible for the following,
  • Masters outside India via exams like GRE
  • Getting a work visa for migrating abroad
  • MBA in India and abroad via exams like CAT & GMAT
  • Government Jobs like IAS, IPS via exams like UPSC

BITS Pilani - Established in 1964 and granted the status of Institute of Eminence by the Indian Government in 2018, offers its online Bachelors of Science (B.Sc.) in Computer Science exclusively through Coursera.
About This Degree
This online degree program runs independently and SST students can pursue this degree concurrently with the Scaler School of Technology's residential undergraduate program. Does this online degree hold the same equivalence as conventional ?
As per Regulation 22 of the UGC (Open and Distance Learning Programmes and Online Programmes) Regulations, 2020, all undergraduate and postgraduate degrees, awarded through Open and Distance Learning mode and/or Online mode by Indian Higher Educational Institutions, recognised by UGC, are treated as equivalent to the corresponding awards of the degrees/diplomas offered through conventional mode.
